educational history

This Sankey diagram tracks how my educational career led to what I know today. When designing my own course, I sat down to trace the prerequisite knowledge that I'd had to acquire before I'd learned the subject I was teaching. I realized that there were layers upon layers of material that I'd practically forgotten about. This visualization was an effort to see how specific levels of expertise evolved out of different inputs. Read More
